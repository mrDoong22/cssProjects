Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light Caesar salad dressing.

Preparation

  1. Prepare the chicken by seasoning it with rosemary, garlic salt and pepper.

  2. Put the quinoa to a boil, then roast the chicken in olive oil until golden brown and 165 degress in the center.

  3. Toss quinoa, chicken, kale, and Caesar dressing until coated.

  4. Add all the parmesan your heart desires, and croutons if you're into that.

Ingredients

1 cup of kale
2 tbsp of Caesar salad dressing
1 cup of quinoa
1 tbsp of garlic salt
2 tbsp of olive oil
1 tbsp of pepper
1 chicken breast
1 tbsp of rosemary
